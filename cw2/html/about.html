Who am I?

I'm a 20 year old student with a background in background in C, Java, Python and Visual Basic. I dabble in jQuery, Angular, Node and PostgreSQL too. I'm currently enrolled on a BSc Computer Science degree at the University of Leeds after achieving an overall distinction on my level three computing course. Programming is central to my life, it is my passion and creative outlet. I'm a passionate and driven perfectionist and as such you can be rest assured that I am more than happy to take on what ever challenge you need met. Outside of programming I'm a music obssessed avid cyclist, gamer and non-fiction consumer. See more.

What drew me to programming?

It was during the second year of my A-level studies that I began to teach myself HTML, I later proceeded to study CSS and JavaScript thereafter. The small learning curve and immediate results that HTML and CSS provided as markup languages acted as a brilliant introduction to coding, but it was JavaScript that gave rise to my fervour for programming later branching out to other languages such as Visual basic, Python and Java and C.

Bringing more than code to the table.

The subjects I studied at A-level; Anthropology, English Literature and Language, and Psychology have sculpted the individual I am today and, whilst not directly relating to computing or its disciplines, each subject has had a significant bearing on how I have learned, thought and engaged throughout my independent and course-driven studies.
